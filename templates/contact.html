Frequently Asked Questions

How does synchronized playback work? +

WatchParty uses WebSocket technology to instantly broadcast play, pause, and seek events to all viewers in the room. When anyone controls the video, everyone else's player syncs automatically.

What video formats are supported? +

We support MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, WebM, WMV, FLV, and M4V. For best compatibility across all browsers, MP4 with H.264 encoding is recommended. Subtitles are supported in SRT and VTT formats.

Is WatchParty free to use? +

Yes! WatchParty is completely free. Create an account, start a room, and invite your friends. No hidden fees or subscriptions required.

How many people can join a watch party? +

There is no hard limit on the number of viewers. Our WebSocket architecture can handle multiple concurrent connections. Performance depends on your server resources.
